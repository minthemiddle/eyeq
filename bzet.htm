Stevie B-Zet (sometime spelled Be-Zet) (Steffen Britzke) is well known for his collaboration swith Sven Vaeth. They have done the project "Astral Pilot", a movie soundtrack for "Der Kalte Finger" and many other things together.

A.C. Boutsen (Matthias Hoffmann) also collaborated with Sven Vaeth, for example he has co-written and co-produced the late "Off" productions and they have the project "Metal Master". 

Other projects Stevie B-Zet and A.C. Boutsen are involved in: 

  • "Zyon" (Stevie B-Zet & A.C. Boutsen co-produced and co-written most of the tracks) 
  • "Vernon" (Stevie B-Zet & A.C. Boutsen co-produced and co-written most of the tracks) 
  • "Reinald Goetz" (Stevie wrote and produced the music for one CD) 
  • "Brainchild" (A.C. Boutsen' olo project) 
  • "Cygnus X." (A.C. Boutsen & Ralf Hildenbeutel) 
  • "Odys(s)ee of Noises" (I'm not sure who exactly is this project. A.C. Boutsen, Stevie B-Zet & Ralf Hildenbeutel are involved, but not everyone at all productions) 
  • "Schallbau" (probably A.C. Boutsen, Stevie B-Zet & Ralf Hildenbeutel. Unconfirmed)
For more details about Harthouse and Eye Q releases take also a look at Joerg Zdarsky' sHarthouse Page.
