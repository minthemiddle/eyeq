Trance

It was originally defined a the monotone and quiet kind of Techno. The use of pad supported the effect to dance to trance. But nowadays relaxed techno combined with pads is classified as Trance. Eye Q used to be the trancelabel with the greatest influence. Song slike Zyon' s"No Fate", Vernon' s"Wonderer", Sonic Infusion' s"Magnificia" or Cygnus X.' s"Superstring" are counted as real classics and still "work" well, very well. But there are other artists, who formed Trance to one of the best kind of music: 
There's  Paul van Dyk, who composed f.e. Vision of Shiva "How much can you take" together with Cosmic Baby, or fabulous remixes of Humate "Love Stimulation" and Joe T. Vanelli' s"Play with the voice"; then of course Cosmic Baby himself, a musician and artist born in Nuremberg (like me!): The Compilation "Tranceformed from Beyond" compiled by Cosmic Baby and Mijk van Dijk was the first Trancecompilation ever. And there was and is Talla 2XLC the father of the Frankfurt Technoclub ("X-Tacy"). DJ DAG, also from Frankfurt, used to be one of the best Trance DJ, since he left Germany to live at his relatives in the USA. There are so many important labels, but I haven't got enough time to describe each of them (if you have, just let me know!):

  • Bonzai Record from Belgium ("The House of House")
  • R&S Records from Belgium (C.J.Bolland "Camargue")
  • M.F.S. Record from Berlin (Paul van Dyk, Cosmic Baby)
  • Suck me Plasma from Frankfurt (Talla 2XLC)
    ... etc., etc. ...
