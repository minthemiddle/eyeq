Ziemlich schnelle Musik, treibend, rund in den Baessen, schon auch heftig in den Sounds; und doch durch die breiten orchestralen und chorischen Flaechen und exponierten Melodien zugleich freundlich gemacht, herzergreifend und uplifting, nach oben hin offen und oeffnend [...] according to Rainald Goetz

A few year sago, strictly speaking in 1992, Sven Vaeth, Matthias Hoffman aka A.C.Boutsen and Svens' manager Heinz Roth created a label, which has been the best Trancelabel ever: Eye Q
Many artists like Stevie B-Zet and A.C. Boutsen aka Veron aka Zyon aka Brainchild etc., Ralf Hildenbeutel with all his projects, f.e. Earth Nation, all Sven Vaeth releases and Cygnus X. and many more released on Eye Q.

The office of Eye Q had been in Offenbach next to Frankfurt until 1996, when the whole Eye Q environment moved to Berlin.

But a few month later, Eye Q got bankruptcy and in 1997 the copyright for the names Harthouse, Eye Q and Recycle or Die was bought by the German company Undercover Music Group. Eye Q UK and Harthouse used to exist until today, but the homepage of Eye Q.com was overtaken by a computer organization, but everyone I asked, don't know what really happened to them.
